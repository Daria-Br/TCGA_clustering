#RMST (Restricted mean survival time) "RMST is the average time free from an event up until a milestone time point, 
#a numeric expression of the area under the Kaplan-Meier survival curve (1,2,4,10). The comparison of RMSTs between treatment groups, 
#the RMST difference (ΔRMST), is the mean absolute difference of event-free time associated with a therapy." or in this case the gene expression cluster
#https://doi.org/10.1016/j.jchf.2020.07.005
